# SANGKURIANG Compliance & Regulatory Documentation

## Overview

SANGKURIANG is a decentralized funding platform for Indonesian cryptography projects. This document outlines the regulatory compliance framework and requirements for operating within Indonesian jurisdiction.

## Regulatory Framework

### 1. Financial Services Authority (OJK) Compliance

#### Crowdfunding Regulations
- **Regulation**: OJK Regulation No. 37/POJK.04/2018 on Information Technology-Based Crowdfunding Services
- **Applicability**: Platform operations involving funding activities
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Registration with OJK for crowdfunding platform operations
  - Maximum funding limit per project (Rp 2 billion)
  - Investor protection measures
  - Risk disclosure requirements
  - Regular reporting to OJK

#### Payment Services
- **Regulation**: Bank Indonesia Regulation No. 23/6/PBI/2021 on Payment Services
- **Applicability**: E-wallet integration (OVO, GoPay, DANA)
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Partnership with licensed payment service providers
  - KYC (Know Your Customer) procedures
  - Anti-money laundering (AML) compliance
  - Transaction monitoring and reporting

### 2. Ministry of Communication and Information Technology (Kominfo)

#### Electronic System Operators
- **Regulation**: Kominfo Regulation No. 5/2020 on Private Electronic System Operators
- **Applicability**: Platform as electronic system provider
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Registration as Electronic System Operator (PSE)
  - Data localization requirements
  - User data protection
  - Content moderation policies

### 3. Investment Coordinating Board (BKPM)

#### Foreign Investment
- **Regulation**: Presidential Regulation No. 10/2021 on Investment Business Fields
- **Applicability**: Foreign investor participation
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Foreign ownership limitations
  - Investment reporting requirements
  - Sector-specific restrictions

## Compliance Implementation

### KYC/AML Procedures

#### User Verification Levels
1. **Basic Level** (Rp 0 - 10 million funding limit)
   - Name, email, phone number verification
   - ID card (KTP) upload
   - Selfie with ID verification

2. **Enhanced Level** (Rp 10 - 100 million funding limit)
   - Address verification
   - Source of funds declaration
   - Enhanced due diligence for PEPs (Politically Exposed Persons)

3. **Premium Level** (Above Rp 100 million funding limit)
   - Bank account verification
   - Income verification
   - Comprehensive background checks

#### Anti-Money Laundering Measures
- Transaction monitoring for suspicious patterns
- Automated risk scoring system
- Suspicious transaction reporting to PPATK (Financial Transaction Reports and Analysis Center)
- Sanctions screening against international lists

### Data Protection & Privacy

#### Personal Data Protection
- **Regulation**: Law No. 27/2022 on Personal Data Protection
- **Implementation**:
  - Explicit consent for data collection
  - Data minimization principles
  - User rights to access, correct, and delete data
  - Cross-border data transfer restrictions
  - Data breach notification procedures

#### Cryptography Export Controls
- **Regulation**: Trade Minister Regulation No. 12/2020
- **Requirements**:
  - Classification of cryptographic strength levels
  - Export licensing for certain cryptographic implementations
  - Technology transfer restrictions

### Project Validation Requirements

#### Technical Standards
- NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) compliance for cryptographic algorithms
- ISO/IEC 27001 for information security management
- OWASP security guidelines for web applications

#### Open Source Requirements
- Mandatory open-source licensing for funded projects
- Code transparency and auditability
- Community contribution guidelines
- Intellectual property compliance
